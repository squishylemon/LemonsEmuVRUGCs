Apple II

Version: 1.0

Apple II, one of the first personal computers originally released back in 1977, is now available within emuVR. At the moment, it is just one object, but maybe I'll end up making them all movable so you can arrange your Apple II in any order. This mini pack also contains a shader and a core for the Apple II. The shader makes the monitor have its realistic '80s monochrome green phosphor display. If you don't know how to install it, click [Not Yet Set Up]. The core is a dedicated Apple II core called AppleWin and is used over MAME as it forces only Apple II media to not break immersion.

Warning: This UGC requires adding 'Apple II = "applewin_libretro"' to your custom_media.text
