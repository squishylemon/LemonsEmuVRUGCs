Commodore 64 [Mega Pack]

Version: 1.03

My commodore 64 pack I made for my father, contains : Media Types C64 Cartridge C64 Cassette C64 Floppy Disc Systems C64 Cartridge C64 Cassette C64 Floppy Disc Screens C64 Screen Props Damaged C64 Box New C64 Box C64 Joystick This modpack took me a whole day and cost me $20. Also, it's my first console/screen/media mod. If there are any issues, please let me know, and I'll try to fix them asap. There are some minor lighting bugs with the models, but I ain't no modeler, and all I know is they were caused by trying to keep the polycounts low. Speaking of polycounts, the floppy disc is very high (4k), and atm I can't find a way to lower this. If anyone could, please do, and I'll credit you! Anyways, please enjoy this C64 pack! PS Systems had to be separated. You can't spawn a C64 and a floppy drive separately. Sozz.
